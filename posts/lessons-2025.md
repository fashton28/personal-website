This year taught me more than I expected. Here are the lessons that stuck.

## Shipping beats perfection

The projects I'm most proud of this year are the ones I shipped fast and iterated on — not the ones I spent months polishing before anyone saw them.

## Say no more often

Every yes is a no to something else. I got better at protecting my time and energy for the things that actually matter.

## Write more, consume less

Writing forced me to clarify my thinking in a way that reading never did. Even if nobody reads it, the act of writing is worth it.
